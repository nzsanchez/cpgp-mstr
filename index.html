Support Funding & Service Level Guidance

This support path is strictly reserved for existing production reports that were previously functional but are currently experiencing technical failures or data discrepancies. All troubleshooting and restoration efforts for these assets are fully covered under Ongoing Maintenance (OGM) and Sustainment Funding, meaning no incremental billing is required. However, the timeline for resolution is dictated by business impact and team capacity; "Critical" status is only granted if a designated business tester is available for real-time triage. Issues without an active tester or those that have existed for extended periods will be queued into the standard sustainment backlog for resolution.

Incremental Request Details

Incremental Request & Funding Policy

An Incremental Request is formally defined as any requirement for brand-new reports, additional data columns, new data views, or supplemental logic that does not exist in the production environment today. Because these requests represent an expansion of the established platform scope, they fall outside the boundaries of standard maintenance and require New Funding Approval before work can commence. Regardless of the requester’s organizational level, every incremental project must undergo a formal discovery phase to determine the necessary resource allocation and budget requirements. Once submitted, the DPP team will review the business case and provide a comprehensive effort estimate to facilitate the funding approval process.

Platform & Cloud Support

Environment & Infrastructure Policy

This specialized support channel is dedicated to environment-level infrastructure, MicroStrategy Cloud performance, and broad system accessibility issues. It is intended for users experiencing connectivity failures, platform-wide latency, or specific administrative requirements related to the cloud environment itself. While infrastructure maintenance is a core service provided by the DPP team, any major environmental upgrades or custom architectural changes may require further review for strategic alignment. Please provide a detailed account of the environment behavior to ensure our engineering leads can accurately assess the system health and prioritize the stability of the platform.
